Love Me True Rescue Redesign

One of my projects in my UI/UX bootcamp was aimed at redesigning a local nonprofit website to help them get even better at doing what they do. My group and I searched for one that we were all passionate about and ended up choosing a cat rescue shelter named Love Me True Rescue. This nonprofit is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and finding forever homes for felines in need in the Ponte Vedra Beach, FL area. We were not able to get a response after reaching out to them, but we chose to move forward with the design challenge either way.

Research

  • Plan: Conduct 5 user interviews with candidates that are interested in animal adoption and/or have experience with nonprofit animal shelter organizations. Semi-structured interview that includes at least 10 open-ended and probing questions. Affinity diagram to identify insights.
